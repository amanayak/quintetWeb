CARE
Caring for your clothes is caring
for the environment. Lower temperature
washes and delicate spin cycles
are gentler on garments and help
to protect the colour, shape, and
structure of the fabric. Furthermore,
they reduce the amount of energy used
in care processes.

Clothing Care Guide
